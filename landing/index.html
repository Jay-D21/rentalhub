What if the item gets damaged? +
Security deposit covers minor damage. For larger issues, AI compares before/after photos and resolves fairly. You can also opt into the 6% Protection Plan for full coverage.
How do I know the person is legit? +
Every user is Aadhaar-verified via DigiLocker before their first transaction. You always see their full name, photo, and trust score (0–100) before accepting any booking.
What can't be listed? +
Private vehicles (white-plate) are not allowed — Motor Vehicles Act requirement. Consumables, weapons, and prescription items are also prohibited. Everything else is welcome.
When do I get paid as a lender? +
Payment releases T+2 after the renter confirms the return. Goes directly to your registered bank account. No manual action needed from you.
Is there a fee to list? +
Listing is completely free. We take 12% only when a rental successfully completes. No subscription, no upfront cost, no hidden charges.
